## CONTENT OPTIMIZATION GUIDELINES

### SEO Best Practices for Each Article:
1. **Title Optimization**: Include primary keyword within first 60 characters
2. **Meta Description**: 150-160 characters with call-to-action
3. **Header Structure**: H1 (title), H2 (main sections), H3 (subsections)
4. **Keyword Density**: 1-2% for primary keyword, natural placement
5. **Internal Linking**: Link to 2-3 related articles
6. **Image Optimization**: Alt text with keywords, compressed images
7. **Social Sharing**: Optimized for Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp

### Engagement Tactics:
1. **Breaking News Alerts**: Push notifications for urgent stories
2. **Social Media Teasers**: Create engaging social media posts
3. **Email Newsletter**: Weekly roundup for subscribers
4. **Comment Engagement**: Respond to reader comments within 2 hours
5. **Polls and Surveys**: Interactive content for engagement

### Performance Metrics to Track:
1. **Page Views**: Target 10,000+ daily page views
2. **Time on Page**: Target 2+ minutes average
3. **Bounce Rate**: Keep below 60%
4. **Social Shares**: Target 100+ shares per major story
5. **Email Subscribers**: Grow by 5% weekly
6. **Search Rankings**: Track top 20 keywords daily
